1.14 Parts of CS covered under different licenses

Some parts of CrystalSpace are not covered under LGPL license, you might have to consider this, depending on how you intend to distribute your finished product.

  • map2cs, the Quake MAP to Crystal Space converter in `apps/import/map2cs'

    Licensed under the GNU General Public License which can be downloaded at:

    http://www.fsf.org/licenses/gpl.txt

  • movierecorder plugin

    Licensed under the GNU General Public License.

In addition, some plugins may need to link to external libraries covered under a license other than the LGPL.

  • None yet.
