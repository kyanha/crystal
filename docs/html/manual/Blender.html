5.5 Making Levels and Models in Blender

Blender is one of the most popular free 3D modellers available and it is supported by Crystal Space. You can find Blender here:

http://www.blender3d.org

For making levels and models you can use the io_scene_cs exporter. io_scene_cs is an add-on for Blender 2.59 and later releases designed to export worlds and models to CrystalSpace. This is an exporter that integrates nicely with Blender (with a full GUI).

It exports:

  • Blender meshes as cs animesh or genmesh factories
  • groups of objects as genmesh factories
  • materials
  • textures
  • morph targets
  • skeletons
  • bone influences
  • animations
  • sockets

You can download io_scene_cs and read the manual from underneath sections.
