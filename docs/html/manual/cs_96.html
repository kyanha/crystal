5.5.7 Step 7: Adding Another Room

A single room is not very interesting, and the ceiling is too high as well.

So first we select every one of the 4 brushes (blocks) that form the walls and reduce the height, then we move the ceiling lower.

Then we duplicate the floor. To do this, we select the floor. Then hold down the SHIFT key and move the floor to the position of the second room. Do the same for the ceiling and the walls.

Now we form a corridor between the two rooms. We duplicate another floor and ceiling, but we reduce the size, so it fits between the rooms.

Now add the walls too.

To make a hole into the corridor, we could use the carve tool of Worldcraft, that lets us carve brushes out of other brushes. I do not recommend doing this, because the results of that operation is not always what you expect. You should only use it, if building the resulting objects out of single brushes is too difficult. In our case it is really simple to avoid. We replace the wall towards the corridor with three blocks, one left of the corridor, one right, and one on top.
