What it's about

I'm making a multiplayer action role-playing game. Currently this application just tests a few ideas:
  • multiple views for split-screen play
  • sky box / environment map / background
  • animated 3d characters

Controls

Esc  exit program

1  show run action
2  show stand action
3  show pain action
4  show death action
5  show attack action
6  show jump action

Upper view

up arrow     look up
down arrow   look down
left arrow   look left
right arrow  look right
insert       roll left
delete       roll right
home         look up and left
end          look down and left
page up      look up and right
page down    look down and right

Lower left view

w  look up
s  look down (also 'x')
a  look left
d  look right
r  roll left
f  roll right
q  look up and left
z  look down and left

c  look down and right

Lower right view

i  look up
k  look down (also ',')

y  roll left
h  roll right

.  look down and right

Known Bugs

  • Converting 24 bit images to 8 bit palettes is very slow and TXTMODE=24bit doesn't work for non-lightmapped polygons. The skybox uses 24 bit JPEG images again so it takes a long time to start in 16 or 8 bit.
  • Floating point exceptions in PreparePolygonFX, pol2d.cpp, lines 169 and 173.
  • Edges of sky box polygons visible because of sloppy texture alignment code that allows some wrapping of the non-tiling textures.
  • Some sprite polygons vanish when they cross the view plane.
