D.2 Portions of Crystal Space Covered Under Different Licenses

Some portions of CrystalSpace are not covered by LGPL license. You might have to consider this, depending on how you intend to distribute your finished product. However, this is usually not problematic, as the modular design of CrystalSpace allows to simply exclude the plugins in question.

  • map2cs, the Quake MAP to Crystal Space converter in `apps/import/map2cs'

    Licensed under the GNU General Public License. See section GNU General Public License.

  • movierecorder plugin

    Licensed under the GNU General Public License. See section GNU General Public License.

In addition, some plugins may need to link to external libraries covered under a license other than the LGPL.
